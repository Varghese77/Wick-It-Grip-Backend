How to Buy

An individual skin costs $15 if the buyer wants to pick one up locally at the University of Washington Campus. Otherwise we charge an additional $6.70 shipping fee for a total order of $21.70. There are three ways to place an order...

Buy Online

There are two ways to buy online. First, you can place an order though Venmo to our account @wick-itgrip. The second way to place an order is to order though our PayPal link.

In either case you should be able to enter a text note. We need to following information: your full name, the console you want a skin for, your email address, and your address.

Remember that this method requires an extra $6.70 for shipping. If there is any transactional error we will refund you the full amount. We will also send you a confirmation email within 24 hours.

Pay and Pick Up Locally (Preferred)

You have the option to arrange a time for buying a skin in person. We allow for payment/pickup anywhere on the University of Washington Seattle Campus during 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m.

You can also pay with cash or a card if you plan to buy locally. To start this process please email us at wickitgrip@gmail.com or send us a message below saying that you are interested in buying a skin on campus. We will send you a confirmation email within 24 hours.

Place a Custom Order

We also allow the use of a custom logo and possibly engravings. If you wish to pursue this option please send us an email at wickitgrip@gmail.com or send us a message below saying that you are interested in placing a custom order. We will follow up within 24 hours to work out the details and arrange for shipping or local pickup.
