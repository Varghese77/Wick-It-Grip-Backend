Hello There

We Are Wick-it Grip


We are a start-up based in Seattle who want to help you get an edge over your opponents. As gamers ourselves, we know how sweat can take you out of your game so we created a skin to solve this problem. Our skin for the PlayStation 4 and Xbox One controllers uses a proprietary combination of neoprene and nylon to help create the ultimate gaming experience.

Let's get down to it

Why you should choose our skin over others!

Sweat Reducement

Our skin was designed specifically to reduce the impact of sweat on gaming. The nylon on the upper layer helps to prevent sweat and hand grease from getting on your controller allowing you to game for hours.

Grip Enhancement

By using neoprene for its bottom layer our skin stays in place and has zero slippage. In addition, neoprene proves a minimal cushion allowing you to grip the controller without discomfort while the nylon provides a nice texture.

Slim Design

Created from a simple 1.5mm later of neoprene and a super thin upper layer of nylon, our skin has a minimal impact on the shape of the controller and has a natural feel when placed on the controller.

Custom Materials

After months of research and development, our team was able to decide upon a custom combination of materials, 1.5mm of neoprene combined with nylon, to create the ultimate sweat wicking skin.
