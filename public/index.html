who We are

We are a group of enthusiatic University of Washington students bringing new grip technology to gaming. As gamers ourselves, we know how sweat can take your head out of the game. We decided to create a controller skin to solve this problem. Our skin is unmatched in its comfort and grip, even when faced with moisture.

Moisture Elimination

Our skin was designed specifically to reduce the impact of sweat on gaming ability.

Grip Enhancement

The cusion of the skin provides a strong hold on the controller and lasting comfort.

Slim Design

Form fitting and sleek design maintains the natural feel of the controller without getting in the way of the controls.

Customized Look

Our original black color and hex design upgrade the look of your controller with new colors and designs on the way.
